About me

Travelling
One of my hobbies is to travel. I believe that everyone should have the opportunity to explore the world and experience its wonders firsthand. Traveling opens our minds, broadens our horizons, and fosters a deeper understanding and appreciation for the diversity of our planet. I am incredibly grateful for the opportunity to travel and explore the world. There’s something incredibly enriching about stepping into unfamiliar territories, experiencing different cultures, and immersing myself in the beauty of our world. One of the greatest joys of traveling is the precious opportunity to create meaningful memories with my family, whether it's wandering through vibrant markets, sampling local cuisine, or simply marveling at breathtaking landscapes together. Over the years, I’ve been fortunate enough to visit countries like Thailand and Japan, each offering its own unique blend of history, culture, and natural wonders. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the serene temples of Tokyo, every destination has left an indelible mark on my heart and expanded my perspective in ways I never imagined.
Shoe collecting
Shoe collecting is one of my interests, one that I find both exciting and rewarding. The thrill of the hunt drives me as I meticulously scour online platforms and marketplaces in search of rare and unique shoes. Not only do I enjoy adding to my personal collection, but I also appreciate the opportunity to turn a profit through strategic purchases. Finding shoes at a bargain price that I can either resell for a profit or add to my own wardrobe brings an added layer of satisfaction to the hunt. It's a delicate balance of passion and pragmatism, where every acquisition holds the potential for both personal enjoyment and financial gain. Each successful find brings a sense of accomplishment and fuels my passion for the hobby. From the quality of materials to the attention to detail, I admire the artistry that goes into creating footwear that transcends mere utility to become objects of desire.
Cycling
Cycling with friends is another cherished hobby of mine. There's something invigorating about hitting the open road or navigating scenic trails on two wheels, surrounded by good company and breathtaking scenery. Whether it's a leisurely ride through the urban concrete jungle or navigating a challenging path, each cycling adventure is an opportunity to connect with friends, push our limits, and create lasting memories together. Cycling with friends isn't just about the physical activity; it's about fostering bonds, strengthening friendships, and enjoying the simple pleasures of life in motion. From the exhilaration of conquering steep climbs to the satisfaction of coasting down scenic descents, every ride is a reminder of the joy and freedom that cycling brings. Together, we explore new routes, discover hidden gems, and embrace the beauty of the world around us, one pedal stroke at a time.
My values: Honesty
Honesty is a cornerstone of my values, guiding my actions and decisions in every aspect of life. I firmly believe that being truthful and transparent builds trust and integrity, not only within oneself but also in relationships and communities. Honesty lays the foundation for meaningful connections, fostering open communication and mutual respect. When we uphold honesty as a core principle, we cultivate authenticity and accountability, creating a culture of integrity where individuals feel empowered to be their true selves. Whether it's admitting mistakes, expressing opinions candidly, or honoring commitments, practicing honesty is essential for building strong, healthy relationships based on trust and respect. Moreover, honesty extends beyond personal interactions to encompass professional ethics and societal responsibilities. As such, honesty is a very important value to me.
